    Major Functions

    Major Functions

    1. 1. Guidance of and recommendations to our members to observe Credit-Specialized Financial Business Act.
    2. 2. Recommendation and guidance on the member companies for the matter of customer relations and consumer protections
    3. 3. Submission of business reports on behalf of our members and analysis of their financial status
    4. 4. Consultation with our members' customers and handling of their general inquiries
    5. 5. Exchange of credit information among our members
    6. 6. Administration of information on credit card merchants
    7. 7. Survey and research for the development of credit-specialized financial business and credit-specialized financial companies
    8. 8. Education for the employees of credit-specialized financial companies
    9. 9. Business for the public relations of credit-specialized financial business
    10. 10. Other miscellaneous functions to achieve the Association's goals
